HERO XPULSE 200 MILAGE AND SPECIFICATIONS

The HERO XPULSE 200 specifications and features includes a 200 cc engine with 18.4 PS power for stress-free Cruising, 21" front wheels and 18" rear wheels with dual purpose tyres, 200 mm high ground clearance, precise power delivery through programmed fuel injection (FI) technology, dual purpose tyres, turn-by-turn navigation, etc. Also the HERO XPULSE 200 comes with full LED headlamps as well as smartphone connectivity with call alerts. The Hero Xpulse 200 engine is fuel efficient with an air-cooled, 4-stroke, 2-valve, single-cyclinder engine that makes 13.5 KW/18.4 PS at 8,000 rpm and a peak torque0f171 Nm at 6,500 rpm. It comes with a 5-speed transmission, a suspension of 37 mm telescopic front and a 10-step rider - adjustable rear monoshock. The bike has a 21 inch and 18 inch sppoked wheels at the front and rear respectivel. It comes with discs at the front and rear paired with single-channel brakes.
